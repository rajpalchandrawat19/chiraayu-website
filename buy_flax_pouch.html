Health & Usage Guide

Cardio Support

High levels of **Omega-3 ALA** help maintain healthy cholesterol and blood pressure.

Gut Health

The soluble and insoluble **Fiber** promotes regular and smooth bowel movements.

Antioxidant Rich

Contains powerful **Lignans** which act as antioxidants and support hormonal balance.

Usage: Must Grind

For maximum nutrient absorption, Flax Seeds **must be ground** before eating.

Smoothie Boost

Ground flax is undetectable in smoothies but delivers a major nutrition punch.
